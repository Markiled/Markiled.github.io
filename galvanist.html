About the Game

Game Concept

The Galvanist is still just an idea that has yet to be solidified and developed. Sometime during late 2018, I had the idea for making a game with puzzles and bosses with mechanics that loosely adhere to the concept of “electromagnetism.” This means that the game is supposed to contain puzzles and boss fights that rely on the use of electric currents and magnetic attraction and repulsion. I started thinking about potential storylines and characters and the protagonist that I ended up coming up with turned into Voltaia, who is featured in most of the concept art above. This game is definitely still in its infant stages, and I have only worked on the project here and there. It is currently a solo project, but I may decide to recruit some help in the future!
