About Me

Hey there! My name is Mark-Justin Avila Pareja and I love drawing, playing video games, and making art assets for games! I’ve always been artistically-inclined ever since I was a young child, and drawing has always been one of my hobbies and favorite pastimes. I also grew up playing video games all the time, and I even went to college to learn how to use my passion to make the games that I love so much.

I graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Computer Game Science from the University of California, Irvine, and during my time at UCI, I began to put my talents to use by working on various game development projects. Whether for my job, for my courses, for my clubs -- such as the Video Game Development Club at UCI -- or just for fun, I have taken as many opportunities as I can to hone my skills.
