Linktober 2017

These are the drawings that I made during October 2017! I started participating in the "Linktober" challenge in 2017 and drew the first few with pen and paper, but I was unable to follow through with the whole month due to other responsibilities such as classwork. I went through and redrew them digitally and colored them, except for the last two, which were originally drawn digitally. Find out more about this yearly drawing challenge at linktober.com!

Day 1: Champion
For the first Linktober 2017 theme, I drew the Champions Daruk and Yunobo but in the likeness of Rick and Morty, so I aptly (and lamely) dubbed this piece "Darick and Yunorty."

Day 2: Merchant/Shop Owner
This Linktober theme was "Merchant/Shop Owner" so I drew one of the more memorable merchants from the Legend of Zelda franchise as he appears in various games. I call this one "The Beedles."

Day 3: Korok
For this Linktober theme, I drew a couple of my favorite Koroks from the series, Makar and Hestu, swapping instruments and having a little jam session.

Day 4: Boss
I drew the ever-fabulous Demon Lord Ghirahim from The Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword for this Linktober theme.

Day 31: FREE FOR ALL!
I drew this one as a double whammy since I was participating in a Halloween-themed art jam for the Video Game Development Club at my college! I named the piece "Halloween in Lorule."
