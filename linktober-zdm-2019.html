Linktober & ZD Marathon 2019

These are the drawings that I made for Linktober and Zelda Dungeon's collaborative marathon for charity!

Day 1: NPC from Four Swords Adventures
The first day's theme is Four Swords Adventures and I rolled a 3, so I decided to draw Princess Zelda from the game as my NPC! She may look a little concerned, but I'm sure you would be too if you were falling from the sky X)

Day 2: Place from Minish Cap
Since I rolled a 1 today, my Day 2 prompt ended up being "A Place from Minish Cap," so I decided to draw Damp√©'s house in the Royal Valley. I tried testing some stuff out with Facebook's 3D photo feature for this one and it's a shame I don't know how to upload the 3D photo here, but it will be posted on my Facebook timeline.

Day 3: Main Character from Skyward Sword
My prompt for Day 3 of the Zelda Dungeon Marathon was "The Main Character of Skyward Sword" since I rolled a 6, so I drew Link and his lil birb~

Day 4: Enemy from Breath of the Wild
Rolled a 4 for Day 4, so I drew Calamity Ganon from Breath of the Wild!

Day 5: NPC from Twilight Princess
I rolled another 3 for this day, so for my NPC from Twilight Princess I drew Telma and her cat Louise.

Day 6: Place from Wind Waker
I had some major artist's block for this one despite Wind Waker being one of my favorite Zelda games lol. I rolled a 1 again, and the place that I ultimately decided to draw was the Wind Shrine behind Dragon Roost Island. I also drew Zephos and Cyclos sharing a drink to celebrate the restoration of Cyclos' monument!

Day 7: NPC from Ocarina of Time
I thought I would go with another pixel piece for this final ZDM entry! I rolled another 3 and the NPC from Ocarina of Time I chose to draw was Saria, who can be seen here relaxing and playing her ocarina in the Sacred Forest Meadow.
