Random Art

This is where I'll be uploading different works of art that I have made, most of which don't necessarily fall under other categories on the site!

OSRS Twisted League Avatar (2019)
I spent a good chunk of time playing Old School RuneScape's Twisted League gamemode when it came out during the 2019 Holiday period and I drew my player character wearing some of the cosmetic rewards that could be earned from the league.

Link's Awakening Avatar (2019)
I drew myself as Link from Link's Awakening in honor of the release of the remake in 2019.

Voltaia Portrait (2018)
This was the first character concept art that I created for Voltaia, who is the protagonist of a little pet project of mine called "The Galvanist" that is currently in the works! The project is still in its early stages and I have yet to decide exactly what direction I want to take it, but you can see some of the current concept art on the "Games" page of my website!

Link and Makar WIP (2017)
I started this piece shortly after beginning my journey in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, but I never got around to finishing it. I wanted to draw the ghost of Makar the Korok giving a Silent Princess flower to a sorrowful Link garbed in his outfit from Wind Waker.

BotW Self Portrait (2017)
Not much to say about this one. I drew myself but in the art style of Breath of the Wild for use as an avatar and for video thumbnails.

Zamorak Sketch (2015)
This was just a little sketch that I made for one of my clanmates from Zamorak Cult, my clan from the MMORPG "RuneScape."

FUSION Assassins Banner (2015)
I drew this to use as a banner for a club event for FUSION, one of my clubs from college. We were hosting a club-wide game of "Assassins."

FUSION Winter Sweater Design (2014)
This was the design I submitted for a sweater design contest held by FUSION. My design was chosen and I got a free sweater with my design on it!

FUSION Flyer Design (2014)
I made this for a flyer that FUSION handed out during our college club fair that year.

VDGC Art Jam: Hat (2014)

VDGC Art Jam: Bug (2014)

VDGC Art Jam: Lizard (2014)
