Random Art

This is where I'll be uploading different works of art that I have made, most of which don't necessarily fall under other categories on the site!

Voltaia Portrait (2018)
This was the first character concept art that I created for Voltaia, who is the protagonist of a little pet project of mine called "The Galvanist" that is currently in the works! The project is still in its early stages and I have yet to decide exactly what direction I want to take it, but you can see some of the current concept art on the "Games" page of my website!

Linktober 2017 Day 1: Champion
I started participating in the "Linktober" challenge in 2017 and drew the first few with pen and paper, but I was unable to follow through with the whole month due to other responsibilities. I went through and redrew them digitally and colored them, except for the last two, which were originally drawn digitally. For the first Linktober 2017 theme, I drew the Champions Daruk and Yunobo but in the likeness of Rick and Morty, so I aptly (and lamely) dubbed this piece "Darick and Yunorty."

Linktober 2017 Day 2: Merchant/Shop Owner
This Linktober theme was "Merchant/Shop Owner" so I drew one of the more memorable merchants from the Legend of Zelda franchise as he appears in various games. I call this one "The Beedles."

Linktober 2017 Day 3: Korok
For this Linktober theme, I drew a couple of my favorite Koroks from the series, Makar and Hestu, swapping instruments and having a little jam session.

Linktober 2017 Day 4: Boss
I drew the ever-fabulous Demon Lord Ghirahim from The Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword for this Linktober theme.

Linktober 2017 Day 31: FREE FOR ALL!
I drew this one as a double whammy since I was participating in a Halloween-themed art jam for the Video Game Development Club at my college! I named the piece "Halloween in Lorule."

Link and Makar WIP (2017)
I started this piece shortly after beginning my journey in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, but I never got around to finishing it. I wanted to draw the ghost of Makar the Korok giving a Silent Princess flower to a sorrowful Link garbed in his outfit from Wind Waker.
