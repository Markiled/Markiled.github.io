Commissions and Requests

These are a few drawing requests/commissions that I have done over the past several years! I recently started a Patreon where I will be handling paid commissions, you can visit my creator page at patreon.com/markiled!

Ian and Hazel (2019)
My older brother asked me to draw him and his girlfriend a few months before I ever got around to making this piece, but I decided to finally draw it for him recently as a birthday present.

Vick's X-Mas Gift (2017)
This was my first paid commission as far as I can remember. My friend Yasmine asked me to draw her boyfriend along with some of his favorite things.

Attack on Titan Fan Art (2014)
This piece was requested by my younger sister. She is a big fan of the anime "Attack on Titan" and wanted me to draw Levi and Mikasa Ackerman, a couple of her favorite characters from the show, so I drew them in their "chibi" forms with the Colossal Titan looming in the background.
